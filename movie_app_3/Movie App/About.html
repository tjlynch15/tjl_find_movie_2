★ Movie Night ★

My Movie App shows movies now playing in theaters, as well as top rated movies

Click on movie to display movie details

Click on "Theaters" to display movies playing within a 5 mile radius of user's location'

Click on a specific movie to display theaters and times for current date

Click on "Tickets" button to load Fandango webpage to purcase tickets for the selected movie at the selected theater at the selected time"

Click on "Map" to display a map view with a search bar

The search bar is pre-populated with "Theaters near me"

Click on the search icon to display the list of theaters

Select a theater and a pindrop will be visable in the map view

Click on the pin, and then click on the car icon to get directions from Apple maps

Enjoy!
